Tyson Beef Facility Emissions

By: Marriele Robinson

Tyson is currently operating seven C,II facilities. As defined by the EPA, C indiacates beef livestock and II indicates a f acility that utilizes wastewater treatment facilities. This map indicates the facilities location, emissions for 2011 and 2015. Additionally, locations emissions are indicated from highest lowest by gradiating from red to beige.
